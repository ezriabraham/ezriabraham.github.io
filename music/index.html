Music

Drumset & Hand Percussion

I have been playing drums since birth but started learning formally at 10 years old. I have played in all types of settings including: cajon in Jewish worship services, congas in the Caltech Jazz Band, drumset in the Northeastern University Pep Band, and cajon on the streets of Boston, Lisbon, Nice, and Hollywood. Below are some fun memories I have captured over the years. Feel free to reach out if you need a drummer/percussionist for a gig!
